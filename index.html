About the Club

Green & Gold RFC is a competitive and family-friendly rugby club competing across regional leagues. Our mission is to build a culture defined by discipline, respect, and excellence on and off the pitch.

  • Home ground: The Oaks Stadium
  • Training: Mon, Tue & Thu • 18:30
  • Programs: Seniors, U20, Women, Rugby

Honors

  • Women’s League Champions: 2024
  • U20 Development Award: 2022
